英语1

阅读主旨

Read a passage about teenagers using AI tools. Choose the best title and explain your reason in Chinese.

  1. Find repeated key words, not one isolated detail.
  2. Compare the first and last paragraphs.
  3. State why other titles are too narrow.

Answer: The best title should cover both AI help and responsible use.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语2

阅读推断

A story says the boy smiled after returning the lost wallet. Infer what he learned.

  1. Locate the change before and after the event.
  2. Use evidence from actions, not imagination.
  3. Answer with a complete sentence.

Answer: He learned honesty brings inner peace and trust.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语3

任务型阅读

Complete a mind map about healthy habits from a passage.

  1. Scan headings and topic sentences.
  2. Use the same part of speech as the blank requires.
  3. Keep answers short.

Answer: sleep well, exercise regularly, eat less sugar, manage stress.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语4

语篇填空

Fill ten blanks in a passage about a school science fair.

  1. Judge whether each blank needs a noun, verb, adjective or conjunction.
  2. Check tense and subject-verb agreement.
  3. Read again for coherence.

Answer: Answers depend on the passage; grammar and context must both fit.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语5

补全对话

Write a dialogue about inviting a friend to a volunteer activity.

  1. Clarify time, place, activity and response.
  2. Use polite questions.
  3. Make the conversation natural.

Answer: Include invitation, details, acceptance or refusal, and closing.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语6

应用写作

Write an email to introduce a study plan for the last week before the exam.

  1. Start with purpose.
  2. Give two or three concrete actions.
  3. End with encouragement.

Answer: A clear three-paragraph email with correct tense and polite tone.

英语7

图表作文

Describe a chart about students' weekend activities and give advice.

  1. State the main trend first.
  2. Compare two important numbers.
  3. Give one practical suggestion.

Answer: Mention the chart, describe data, and advise balanced time use.

英语8

句子升级

Upgrade simple sentences about reading into a richer paragraph.

  1. Use because, however, what's more.
  2. Avoid very long uncontrolled sentences.
  3. Keep grammar accurate.

Answer: Coherent paragraph with connectors and no major grammar errors.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语9

完形逻辑

Explain how to choose between although, because, so and but in a cloze passage.

  1. Read the relation between two clauses.
  2. Cause uses because, result uses so.
  3. Contrast uses but/although.

Answer: Choose by logical relation, not by translation alone.

高分提醒：答题时要把依据写出来，避免只给结论。

英语10

开放表达

Give three suggestions to a nervous classmate before the exam.

  1. Use should/can/had better.
  2. Be specific.
  3. Keep a supportive tone.

Answer: Review mistakes, sleep early, take deep breaths, believe in yourself.
