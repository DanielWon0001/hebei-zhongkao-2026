英语1

阅读主旨

Passage: More students are using AI tools to check grammar, make plans and search for ideas. AI can save time, but it cannot replace thinking. A student who only copies AI answers may finish homework quickly, yet learn very little. A better way is to ask AI for hints, compare answers and then write in one's own words.
题目：Question: What is the best title? Explain why.

  1. 抓反复出现的关键词：AI tools, thinking, own words。
  2. 文章不是单纯介绍AI，也不是反对AI，而是强调负责任使用。
  3. 标题应覆盖“帮助”和“不能替代思考”两个方面。

Answer: A good title is 'Use AI Wisely'. It covers both the usefulness of AI and the need for independent thinking.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语2

推断题

Passage: Lily found a wallet on the bus. She was late for her piano lesson, but she got off at the next stop and gave it to the driver. That evening, the owner called to thank her. Lily missed ten minutes of practice, but she said, 'I feel lighter than if I had arrived on time.'
题目：Question: What did Lily probably learn?

  1. 先看人物选择：迟到风险和归还钱包之间，她选择诚实。
  2. 再看结尾feel lighter，说明内心轻松。
  3. 推断不能脱离文本，答案应围绕honesty。

Answer: She learned that honesty is more important than convenience and can bring inner peace.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语3

任务型阅读

Passage: To prepare for an exam, students should make a realistic plan, review mistakes, sleep well and keep a calm mind. A plan tells you what to do each day. Mistakes show what you still need to improve. Good sleep helps the brain remember better.
题目：Question: Complete the mind map: Plan, Mistakes, Sleep, Mind.

  1. 从每一句找对应信息。
  2. Plan对应what to do each day。
  3. Mistakes对应need to improve。Sleep对应remember better。Mind对应calm。

Answer: Plan: daily tasks; Mistakes: weak points; Sleep: better memory; Mind: calm attitude.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语4

完形逻辑

Passage: Tom wanted to give up running, ____ his coach asked him to slow down instead of stopping. After two weeks, he could run farther ____ before. He realized progress came from patience.
题目：Question: Fill in the blanks with but, than, because, so.

  1. 第一空前后是转折：想放弃，但教练让他慢下来。
  2. 第二空farther than before是比较结构。
  3. 检查句意是否连贯。

Answer: but; than.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语5

补全对话

Passage: A: Hi, Jack. Our class will visit the old people's home this Saturday. B: Sounds meaningful. What will we do there? A: We will clean rooms and sing songs for them. B: ______ A: At 8:00 at the school gate. B: OK. ______
题目：Question: Complete B's two sentences.

  1. 第一空根据回答At 8:00 at the school gate，应该问时间地点。
  2. 第二空在对话结尾，应表示同意参加。
  3. 语言要自然礼貌。

Answer: When and where shall we meet? / I will be there on time.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语6

应用写作

Prompt: Your friend is nervous before the exam. Write an email to give advice. Include review, sleep and confidence.
题目：Question: Write the outline and key sentences.

  1. 开头表达理解：I know you are worried.。
  2. 主体给三条建议：review mistakes, sleep early, take deep breaths。
  3. 结尾鼓励：I believe you can do well.。

Answer: Use a three-part email: comfort, three suggestions, encouragement. Keep sentences correct and specific.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语7

图表表达

Passage: A chart shows 45% of students spend weekends on homework, 30% on phones, 15% on sports and 10% on reading.
题目：Question: Describe the chart and give one suggestion.

  1. 先写最大比例：homework takes the largest part。
  2. 再比较phones and sports/reading。
  3. 建议要平衡学习和健康。

Answer: Most students spend weekends on homework, while fewer students read or play sports. They should spend less time on phones and do more exercise.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。

英语8

语法填空

Passage: Last Friday, our school held a science fair. Many students ______ (show) their inventions. I was ______ (surprise) by a small robot. It could pick up bottles and put ______ (they) into the right bin.
题目：Question: Fill the blanks.

  1. Last Friday提示一般过去时，show变showed。
  2. be surprised by为固定用法。
  3. put后代词作宾语，they变them。

Answer: showed; surprised; them.

英语9

阅读细节

Passage: The library opens at 8:30 on weekdays and 9:00 on weekends. Students can borrow three books each time and keep them for two weeks. If they need more time, they can renew the books online once.
题目：Question: How many books can a student borrow and how long can they keep them?

  1. 细节题回原文定位borrow和keep。
  2. 不要把renew后的时间直接当作首次借阅时间。
  3. 回答完整句。

Answer: A student can borrow three books each time and keep them for two weeks.

英语10

开放表达

Passage: A school magazine asks students to write about one habit that helps learning. You choose 'asking questions'.
题目：Question: Write three reasons in English.

  1. 理由一：questions help find weak points。
  2. 理由二：questions make classes active。
  3. 理由三：asking teachers or classmates saves time。
  4. 注意三条理由用完整句。

Answer: Asking questions helps me find what I do not understand. It makes me think more actively. It also helps me solve problems faster with help from teachers or classmates.

高分提醒：阅读题必须回到原文找依据，答案要分点、完整、可对应文本。
